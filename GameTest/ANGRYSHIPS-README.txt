========================================================================
    UBISOFT NEXT 2024  - Angry Ships
========================================================================

 Controls: 	Arrow Keys to Swap Ships / Weapons
		W/S to change azimuth
		Space to Fire
		Backspace to End Turn
		Home to Return to own Ship
		End to Back out of Selected Ship and Choose a New Ship

		LCtrl to Exit to Menu

		Can use Mouse at Edges of screen to scroll
